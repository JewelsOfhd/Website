About Us

Growing up I always looked for unique pieces of jewellry that was not easily found around Pietermaritzburg at affordable prices. This fueled my passion to become an entrepreneur so that I could provide what I once looked for. We at Jewels of H.D. strive to provide the best quality products at unbeatable and most definitely unforgettable prices. We offer a variety of minimalist jewellry pieces for all budgets and welcome all suggestions from our customers and potential customers. This is the start to a beautiful friendship and we hope to live up to it, here at Jewels of H.D. Get shopping! We can't wait to meet you ❤.
